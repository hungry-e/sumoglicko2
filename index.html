The Glicko-2 Rating System

Glicko-2 is a sophisticated evolution of the classic Elo system. While Elo only tracks a single "skill" number, Glicko-2 introduces Rating Deviation (RD) and Volatility to ensure that inactive or unpredictable rikishi don't skew the system.
